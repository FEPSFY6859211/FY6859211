Introduction

Technology advancements in the military have significantly reshaped the battlefield in the modern age, where unmanned autonomous and remote-controlled systems have emerged as game-changers in military operations. Many years ago, the main firepower of militaries used to be the courage of soldiers running into the front lines, risking their lives, today, robotic ground vehicles, autonomous drones, and refined remote controlled systems have redefined the strategy of war and defence. Due to these innovations, it has allowed militaries of the world to enhance their operational efficiency and precision whilst minimising the risk of putting any human life in danger, which therefore allows military personnel to make assessed moves and decision from a safe distance.
